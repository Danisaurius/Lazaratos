Hello there!

I'm Paola Lázaro

Collage saved my life. It allowed me to escape my mind and enter a different realm where matter becomes symbol and everyday life takes on poetic significance. In that universe, there is only the possibility of creation, and creation itself is my freedom.


Each cutout reveals the impossibility of totality and, simultaneously, the power of recomposition. Joining scattered fragments is an act of resistance against chaos and an affirmation that meaning is constructed, not given.


I am a collage maker, musician, and digital marketing strategist. But I inhabit this world of infinite possibilities.

This space is a work in progress. Just like myself.

Open to collaborations and new projects.
